## 🎨 Design Decisions Explained

### Why Navy Instead of Black?
- **#0f0f1e** reduces eye strain compared to pure black (#000000)
- Slightly warmer tone, more professional
- Pairs better with teal accents

### Why Teal?
- **#14b8a6** is professional and tech-forward
- High contrast with navy background
- Associated with trust and confidence
- Popular in modern fintech apps

### Why Space Mono for Headers?
- Monospace conveys technical sophistication
- Matches professional trading platforms
- Excellent for displaying odds/numbers
- Modern yet familiar aesthetic

### Why Subtle Geometric Art?
- Creates visual interest without clutter
- Professional portfolio/agency aesthetic
- Doesn't interfere with content
- 3% opacity keeps focus on data

### Why Cards Instead of Tables?
- Cards group related information
- Better for mobile responsiveness
- More visual appeal
- Easier to scan quickly
- Modern UI best practice
